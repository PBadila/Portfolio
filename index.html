About Me

As a seasoned laboratory chemist and former bulk herbs buyer for a health and wellness store, I have developed a keen sense of curiosity and a passion for exploring the intersections between science and nature. With a solid educational background in both fields, I am now embarking on a new journey as a full stack software engineer.

Beyond my technical expertise, I am also a certified Kemetic Yoga instructor, herbalist, and urban agriculturist. I believe that a well-rounded approach to health and wellness encompasses not only physical fitness, but also mental and emotional well-being. In today's world, specifically as we enter the Age of Aquarius, technology plays a crucial role in facilitating this holistic approach, and I am eager to leverage my skills in programming to continue to uplift and educate others.

Throughout my career, I have honed my problem-solving abilities, both in the lab and as a mother of five children. I am no stranger to navigating complex challenges, and I thrive in fast-paced environments where critical thinking and adaptability are essential.

As I transition into the tech industry, I am excited to bring my unique perspective and skill set to the table. I am confident that my passion for learning, combined with my diverse background, will enable me to make a meaningful impact in the field of software engineering.
